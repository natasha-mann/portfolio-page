ABOUT ME

My Past

I have spent most of my life learning languages - the spoken kind. Spanish, French, and Italian mainly, though I have been known to learn random phrases for any country I visit... I have always been interested in the scientific aspect of language: the grammar, the etymology of words, every aspect of linguistics. I have a very logical and detail orientated mind, which I hope will lend itself very well to coding!

My Present

I am currently a student of the University of Birmingham full-stack web development coding bootcamp. So far, I would say I have a "conversational level" (excuse the language pun!) in both HTML and CSS, including building responsive and accessible websites. By the end of the course I will hopefully have developed a solid understanding of Javascript, have learnt how to work with servers and databases, and will be able to engineer a full-stack web application.

My Future

I am excited about the prospect of continuing to learn and develop my skills even after my bootcamp ends. I am intrigued by all of the different coding languages and frameworks and would love to study more and add more skills to my portfolio. I am confident in my ability to adapt and mould myself to the workings of different companies and would relish the opportunity to start a new career as a software developer.

Travel Companion

Travel Companion is a travel app designed to allow the user to search for a country of their choice, or view a randomly generated country, and find information such as places to see in the capital, health data and a currency converter. The user can add countries to their favourites through local storage to review at a later date.
